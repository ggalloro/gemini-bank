# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is the Gemini Bank application free to use?**

A: Yes, the application is completely free to use.

**Q: How many bank accounts can I create?**

A: You can create as many bank accounts as you need.

**Q: Is my data secure?**

A: Yes, all your data is stored securely, and your passwords are encrypted.

**Q: Can I access my account from multiple devices?**

A: Yes, you can access your account from any device with a web browser and an internet connection.
