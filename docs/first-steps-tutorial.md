# Your First Tutorial

This tutorial will guide you through the process of creating a new account and making your first transaction.

## Step 1: Register a New User

1.  Click on the "Register" button on the top navigation bar.
2.  Fill in your first name, last name, email address, and a password.
3.  Click on the "Register user" button.

## Step 2: Create a New Bank Account

1.  Log in to your account.
2.  Click on the "Create Account" button.
3.  A new bank account will be created for you with a unique account number.

## Step 3: Make a Deposit

1.  Click on the account you want to make a deposit to.
2.  Click on the "New Transaction" button.
3.  Enter a description for the transaction.
4.  Enter the amount you want to deposit.
5.  Select "Deposit" as the transaction type.
6.  Click on the "Make Transaction" button.

## Step 4: Make a Transfer

1.  Click on the account you want to make a transfer from.
2.  Click on the "New Transaction" button.
3.  Enter a description for the transaction.
4.  Enter the amount you want to transfer.
5.  Select "Transfer" as the transaction type.
6.  Click on the "Make Transaction" button.
