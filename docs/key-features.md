# Exploring Key Features

Gemini Bank offers a range of features to help you manage your finances effectively.

## User Registration and Login

*   **What it is:** This feature allows you to create a personal account and securely log in to the application.
*   **How to use it:** Click on the "Register" button to create a new account. Once registered, you can log in using your email and password.

## Account Creation

*   **What it is:** You can create multiple bank accounts to organize your finances.
*   **How to use it:** After logging in, click on the "Create Account" button to generate a new bank account.

## Transactions

*   **What it is:** You can perform two types of transactions: deposits and transfers.
*   **How to use it:** Select the account you want to transact with, click on "New Transaction," and fill in the required details.

## Account Overview

*   **What it is:** This feature provides a summary of your account, including the current balance and a list of all your transactions.
*   **How to use it:** Click on any of your accounts to view its details.
